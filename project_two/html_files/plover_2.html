RemarkA sequence of sedimentary rocks ranging in age from Miocene to Upper Permian was penetrated. The Upper Jurassic section comprised good reservoir sandstone,with Cretaceous and Lower Triassic shale sections providing top and bottom seals. Several minor gas shows were recorded while drilling the Triassic and Permian sections. There were no indications of oil and no fluorescence or cut detected.Plover 2 is located offshore in the Bonaparte Gulf Basin, Western Australia, 31km SW of Plover 1 and 105km SE of Whimbrel 1.
